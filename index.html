Who am I?

My name is Tim,  I'm a 3D generalist who loves movies, videogames, and animation. I was born in 2005 in Ohio, where I currently live. I started learning Blender back in 2019 because I was bored in highschool. Covid-19 spread soon after and it gave me a ton of extra free time at home to learn the basics of working in a 3D space. I tried a variety of different things, from music production to game design, but I always kept coming back to 3D animation and eventually decided to formally learn it after enrolling at the Cleveland Institute of Art. I've made countless friends their and continue to learn so much there. Since coming to CIA, I have learned to use Maya, Unreal Engine, Cascadeur, Substance Painter, Embergen, Nuke, Octane, and many other programs in my journey to become an efficient generalist. I hope to keep learning new programs as well as to refine my skills as a 3D generalist.
